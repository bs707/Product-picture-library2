Beyond a Tool: A Creator's Vision

The Birth of Instant Aesthetics

As a 3D artist focusing on social media, I spent years trapped in a repetitive cycle: rendering, moving to post-processing for CRT effects, realizing the lighting was wrong, and heading back to Blender. **I created SocialCam Master Pro to end this friction.**

I wanted to see the final result while I was still in the creative flow. This add-on started as a personal script to automate my camera bookmarks and resolution presets. It evolved into a powerful Viewport FX engine when I realized that real-time inspiration is the most valuable asset a creator has.
