SocialCam Master Pro

After working in 3D animation for years, I got tired of always having to change the camera aspect ratio by hand. I also noticed that there was no simple "flip aspect ratio" button. So, I developed this plugin! I also added controls for colour and filters that you can use to make simple changes to the visual effects.

I had a great idea while I was working on it: wouldn't it be even better to be able to jump straight to a specific shot like you can with a bookmark? You won't have to scroll endlessly or get lost between multiple images. I added this feature, and it completely changed the way I work.
